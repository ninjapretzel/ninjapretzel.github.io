TapRPG Help


Welcome to TapRPG, and endless CPG (Character Progression Game) that you can bring with you wherever you go on a mobile Android device.

This is a game to play whenever you need to wait on something that takes a really long time, or are otherwise just bored.
Or if you don't have internet to access one of the many timesink games that can only be played with an internet connection.

This is my favorite type of game, a grindy game that has no end, that can just be played on and on and on and on and on. You get the idea

I built this game after being addicted to a similar game, Hentaiverse. Yeah. I know what it sounds like. Its not that.
I still play that game from time to time. It's only fair that I link to it: http://hentaiverse.org
