BHOP SIMULATOR 2013
CONTROLS:
WASD - Move
Space- Jump
C - Crouch
Shift - Sprint
J - Toggle AutoJump
I - Toggle Color Effect
P - Toggle Pause
Esc - Return to menu
Scroll up/down - Change Weapon
1-3 - Switch to weapon
1-M4 | 2-Rocket | 3-Grenades

I apologize if it runs slowly.
How to go fast:
Jump, then move your mouse left and right while holding Forward(W) or another direction to accelerate in that direction.
Jump again when you hit the ground to keep speed. If you can't get the timing, hit 'J' to toggle AutoJump
You can control the height of your jumps as well. Hold down crouch and jump to jump higher. Sprint will let you move faster, but lowers your jump height
