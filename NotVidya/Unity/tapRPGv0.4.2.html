Some changes since last version:
Tweaked some icons to make them look more interesting.
Weapon/Armor skill is now capped based on your level.

Skills have been nerfed, multitarget skills more than single target skills.

Stupid grindy game that I've been working on for Android/iOS.

You constantly gain power as you gain exp and golds/gems to upgrade yourself.
Difficulty modes make it even more grindy- fight stronger monsters for better rewards!
Icon graphics based off of icons from E-Hentai.org's Hentaiverse, which I have been addicted to for the past few months.
