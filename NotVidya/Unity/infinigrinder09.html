Use WASD or Arrows to move. Click to fire towards the mouse
With a controller, move with the left stick and attack with the right (like geometry wars)
Kill enemies, they will drop stuff.
You can't die yet. I figure its more of a pain when you die in a demo so thats off till later
You can equip weapons and armor that you find.
There's already quite a number of possibilities for weapons and armor to be generated.

Shouldn't ever fall through floors when spawning on the field anymore.

V0.3.2
Lots of changes from previous version. SAVING AND LOADING HAS BEEN IMPLEMENTED. However- Unity won't let webplayer load data saved by a different URL- so any saves from the web version are locked to that URL.
This means as I upload new versions, you will have to start over each time on the web.
Standalone saves and loads from the registry, so saves will be able to be loaded between versions.
Added a keyword system to generate areas. Right now its an obvious ripoff of .hack//'s awesome keyword system, but it will be refined into something more unique eventually
Added a 'field' level. This is the 'top level' in an area, and is where you warp to when you enter an area. These are really tiny right now, but will be expanded to be larger
The field areas 'wrap around' - if you keep walking in one direction, you end up back where you started.
Enemies don't spawn infinitly on the field - yet. There are still enemy spawners in the dungeon.
Drop rate is reduced a ton to make items actually valuable.
