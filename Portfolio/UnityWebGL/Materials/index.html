Interactive Materials

Version 0.1.1
Created by Jonathan Cohen, 2017

Instructions

Click the material buttons in the top to select a preset
Use the controls below to change the properties of the material, live, in the browser!
Rotate the camera with WASD or Arrow Keys.
Each Kind of material has its own property list.
Properties such as '_Iterations' and '_Octaves' have a big effect on the resulting quality
'_Seed' can be used to generate many variations of the same texture
